About Blabar

" F@CK, how it's said in Spanish "
-Me, someday.

Sometimes we need to make fast translations but or you have a translator app open and ready to do or you need to open web browser, search for the correct translator and the do the translation. We need a fast and reliable solution and this is BlaBar.

A non-intrusive app that resides on your menu bar and is always ready to make good translations. This could be thanks to the power of DeepL.com one of the actual best translators online due to the use of neuronal networks to make the translations.
